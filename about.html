Why We're Different

Most LLC formation sites are designed to sell you services. We're not.

Here's what makes us different:

  • No upsells: We don't try to sell you registered agent services, operating agreements, or "expedited filing" packages. Our guides are genuinely free.
  • State-verified information: Every guide is based on official state sources. We link directly to Secretary of State websites and government resources.
  • Updated for 2026: LLC filing fees and requirements change. We keep our guides current so you don't get hit with surprise costs.
  • Plain English: No legal jargon. No confusing terminology. Just straightforward explanations anyone can understand.
  • Comprehensive coverage: Filing fees, processing times, annual requirements, registered agent rules, tax considerations — we cover everything you need to know.

Our promise: If you can't find the information you need in our guides, we haven't done our job. And we're constantly improving based on user feedback.

How We Make Money

You might be wondering: "If this is free, how do you keep the lights on?"

Fair question. Here's how we sustain this project:

  • Display advertising: We run non-intrusive ads on the site (Google AdSense). You're not required to click them, and the content is free regardless.
  • Affiliate partnerships: If you choose to use a formation service (like ZenBusiness or LegalZoom) instead of filing yourself, we may earn a small commission. But we never recommend a service just because it pays us. Our guides show you how to file yourself for free.

We don't:

  • Charge for guides or templates
  • Require email signups to view content
  • Hide critical information behind "premium" tiers
  • Sell your data or send spam emails
